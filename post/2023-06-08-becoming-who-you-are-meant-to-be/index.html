Becoming Who You Are Meant to Be

Bernhard Guenther and Laura Matsue

 Posted on June 8, 2023  |  2 minutes  |  283 words  |  Drunken Sage

I have recently come across the work of Bernhard Guenther and Laura Matsue and have been enjoying their podcasts and perspective on the soul awakening/enlightenment journey. This particular podcast episode hits home for myself, as I recently began to understand just how much the false “I” or ego I was actually dealing with.

I spent years after my awakening gorging myself on all the spiritual information I could get my hands on. This began to develop a sense of “specialness”, that I was more awake, more wise, more whatever than those around me. Through the mirror of my wife, who strongly and consistently pointed out the arrogance in which I spoke, I began to meditate on, and reflect on this.

This reflection and seeking led me to this video by Aaron Abke on the “Spiritual Ego”:

After some time of contemplation and reflection,I noticed that my intentions for sharing were not always as wholesome as I had let myself believe. There was definitely a “pay-off” I was getting, primarily in the area of pride.

(We will break down this concept of ego, or the false I in a future article)

Hope you enjoy the podcast, I know I did 😀

Laura and Bernhard discuss the process of Individuation which many people are called to engage in. They talk about the misconceptions, how most people work on the “false self”, what the process entails, and the value of struggle and suffering to become who you truly are and uncover your gifts and purpose. Furthermore, they talk about facing the collective shadow, how the individuation process connects you with Divine Will, how the Left and Right are caught in a shadow dance, and much more.
