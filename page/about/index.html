About me

Just a few non-important details

While details about myself I feel are rather non-important, they might help you to better understand my journey, and see the lens that distorts my viewpoints.

After discovering meditation and practicing it daily over the course of several months, I was blessed to have an experience of the divine. This presence I felt was one of that of overwhelming love, and I was surrounded by a brilliant white light. I have no idea how long this lasted, but it felt I was in a place of complete timelessness. The outside world vanished and seem rather unimportant. Although I could have spent forever in this state of joy and bliss, it did however eventually cease, and I returned to my normal point of awareness.

Prior to this moment, I held a very narrow world view. I considered myself to be a religious person, and a devout believer in Christianity. This caused a shake up in my beliefs and world view, enough that I disavowed religion and began to seek answers outside of it. This lead me to study about Buddhism, Hindu teachings, new age, metaphysics, occult and esoteric teachings. Of ll the materials I consumed in my search for answers, one in particular grabbed hold of me, and became a core for how my view was shaped. This was the RA Material, channeled via L/L Research between 1981 and 1984, over 106 sessions. Personally, I have found many channeled sources to questionable, this however was different. The beings coming through explained things in a fashion that I don’t feel could have originated from beings of our density. After working through the material myself, I began to also listen to the video series by Dr Scott Mandelker , where is breaks down the series in great detail.

The study of the Law of One lead me to many other great teachers and authors that continued to expand my awareness, and develop my current lens. I started following Aaron Abke on YouTube, and really enjoyed many of his insights. Through his videos, he introduced me to the works of Dr David R. Hawkins, which began my next great leap in understanding.

After years of research, looking to confirm my experience and possibly try and recreate it, I can say that I have finally came to a place of peace. My goal is to share what has been revealed to me, and possibly assist others to also find this peace.

I will crate a links page to all the materials I have viewed, and will add to it, in case they are helpful to anyone else. I have always found the way the beings in the Ra material signed off, so I will use that in this blog…

In the love and light of the One infinite creator
